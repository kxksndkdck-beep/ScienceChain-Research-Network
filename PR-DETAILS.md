# Smart Contract Implementation for Research Integrity Platform\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces four comprehensive smart contracts that form the core infrastructure of the ScienceChain Research Network, a blockchain-based platform designed to revolutionize scientific research integrity and collaboration.\n\n## What's New\n\n### 🔐 Research Data Registry (`research-data-registry.clar`)\nA robust contract for managing research data with cryptographic integrity verification.\n\n**Key Features:**\n- **Cryptographic Timestamping**: Immutable proof of data creation with SHA-256 hashing\n- **Data Integrity Verification**: Real-time validation of research data against stored hashes  \n- **Status Management**: Track research through draft, review, published, and verified states\n- **IPFS Integration**: Support for off-chain data storage with on-chain verification\n- **Metadata Storage**: Comprehensive research context and verification tracking\n\n**Core Functions:**\n- `register-research-data`: Register new research with cryptographic proof\n- `verify-data-integrity`: Validate data integrity against stored hashes\n- `update-data-status`: Manage research lifecycle states\n- `get-research-metadata`: Retrieve complete research information\n\n### 👥 Peer Review System (`peer-review-system.clar`)\nTransparent and accountable peer review infrastructure with built-in conflict of interest disclosure.\n\n**Key Features:**\n- **Reviewer Credentials**: Verified expert registration with institutional affiliations\n- **Conflict of Interest Disclosure**: Mandatory transparency in review relationships\n- **Quality Metrics**: Performance tracking and reputation scoring for reviewers\n- **Review Aggregation**: Weighted scoring system for comprehensive evaluation\n- **Transparency**: All reviews are cryptographically signed and verifiable\n\n**Core Functions:**\n- `register-reviewer`: Join as a qualified peer reviewer\n- `submit-review`: Submit comprehensive reviews with conflict disclosure\n- `get-reviewer-credentials`: Access reviewer qualifications and history\n- `calculate-review-score`: Generate weighted review scores\n\n### 🔄 Replication Tracking Network (`replication-tracking-network.clar`)\nGlobal registry for tracking research replication attempts and verifying reproducibility.\n\n**Key Features:**\n- **Replication Registry**: Centralized tracking of all replication attempts\n- **Outcome Documentation**: Detailed recording of replication results\n- **Statistical Analysis**: Success rates, confidence intervals, and effect sizes\n- **Quality Assessment**: Peer review of replication methodologies\n- **Reproducibility Scoring**: Quantitative measures of research reliability\n\n**Core Functions:**\n- `register-replication-attempt`: Register new replication studies\n- `submit-replication-results`: Document replication outcomes\n- `get-replication-history`: Access complete replication records\n- `update-replication-status`: Manage replication lifecycle\n\n### 🏆 Research Integrity Rewards (`research-integrity-rewards.clar`)\nToken-based incentive system promoting quality research practices and community engagement.\n\n**Key Features:**\n- **SCI Token Economy**: Native cryptocurrency for research integrity rewards\n- **Reputation-Based Multipliers**: Enhanced rewards for established researchers\n- **Activity Tracking**: Comprehensive logging of all reward-eligible actions\n- **Tier System**: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum reputation levels\n- **Fair Distribution**: Transparent token allocation based on contribution quality\n\n**Core Functions:**\n- `mint-integrity-tokens`: Issue rewards for verified integrity actions\n- `claim-rewards`: Redeem earned tokens for research activities\n- `calculate-reputation-score`: Generate researcher reputation metrics\n- `distribute-rewards`: Batch token distribution to multiple recipients\n\n##
